Lifeline

Assiting global citizens by identifiying threats and violence in thier community.

Contributing to the well-being of humanity.

Lifeline believes that the world needs to be a safer place.

America, in particular, is witnessing rates of incarceration that are unprecedented. In Paris, the Middle East, and abroad, networks and organizations of people can act in violent, unnatural ways to achive thier objectives.

These ways of conflict resolution damage cutural tolerance, international cooperation, and cause more conflicts among civilians.

Our team is developing a technology that significantly reduces violence, human trafficking, and crime, while simultaneously promoting cooperation from global civilians.

How it Works

Users submit observations about their surroundings through the Lifeline API.

Lifeline's AI platform processes, displays, and organizes the information seamlessly.

Lifeline then transmits this observational data to other users, revealing crime-related information in their community.
